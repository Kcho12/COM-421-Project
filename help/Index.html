Simple activities to help the environment

We have devised 3 simple types of activities for seniors to contribute to the environmental problem.

Recycling Old Medicines

    If you have any unused prescriptions, you can bring it over a Chicago Area Police Station to properly dispose of them any time of the day at these locations.

  • 5101 S. Wentworth Ave.

  • 727 E. 111th St

  • 2452 W. Belmont Ave

  • 3151 W. Harrison St.

  • 5555 W. Grand Ave.

Report Environmental Concerns

    Report illegal dumping and hazardous cleanups to the Chicago department of environment. (312) 744-7672.

    Inspectors are on duty from Monday-Friday 6:00 am to 12:00 am

    Check out the City of Chicago Website for more information on illegal dumping: https://www.chicago.gov/city/en/depts/cdph/provdrs/healthy_communities/svcs/report_an_illegaldumping.html

Vote for a representative that cares about the climate

    BallotPedia provides the results of previous elections and information on future ballots: https://ballotpedia.org/Illinois_elections,_2019

  • You can register to vote online at: https://ova.elections.il.gov/

  • More information on voting can be found at: https://elections.il.gov/InfoForVoters.aspx
