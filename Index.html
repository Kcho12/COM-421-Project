What is Climate change?

What is Climate Change Impact of Climate Change How you can help

The climate is what shapes the environment we live in.

What are the changes?

  • The temperature is getting hotter

  • Flooding is occuring more along the coastline

  • Wildfires are occuring more often

  • Glaciers and Snow covers are melting in the Artic

What is the cause?

The cause is Greenhouse gases which are released by:

  • Burning fossil fuel

  • Deforestation

  • Land-use change

Greenhouse gases prevent heat from leaving Earth's atmosphere. Without greenhouse gases, Earth would not be warm enough to sustain life. Right now, we have too much of these green house gases due to increased human industrial activity within the last century. As a result, the changes in the global temperature are causing negative changes in our climate.
