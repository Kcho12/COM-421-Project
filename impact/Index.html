What are the Impacts of Climate Change?

Human Health

  • More Heat waves increase the chance of heat stress

  • Worsening air quality makes it harder to breathe

  • Warmer temperatures allow rodents and insects to survive more easily and spread dieseases

Water Resources

  • Rain will be more intense leading to an increase flood damage.

  • Increased frequency in Summer droughts mean there will be less water levels in lakes and wetlands

  • Warmer temperatures allows for the growth of blue-green and toxic algae to grow in the Great Lakes reducing water quality

Agriculture and ecosystems

  • Growing seassons are lengthened; however, that also causes an increase in disease-causing pathogens

  • Warmer temperatures cause stress to livestock which reduce meat, milk, and egg production

  • Forrests are afflicted by wildfires, droughts, and insect outbreaks
